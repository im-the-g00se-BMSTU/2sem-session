### Основные свойства неопределенного интеграла
1. $\int (f(x) \pm g(x))dx = \int f(x)dx \pm \int g(x)dx$
2. $\int af(x)dx = a\int f(x)dx$
3. $\left(\int f(x)dx\right)'=f(x)$
4. $\int F'(x)dx = \int \frac{d(F(x))}{dx}dx = \int d(F(x)) = F(x)+C$
### Таблица основных интегралов
1. $\int dx = x+C$
2. $\int x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1}+C$
3. $\int \frac{dx}{x}=\ln |x|+C$
4. $\int \frac{dx}{1+x^2}=\operatorname{arctg}x+C$
5. $\int \frac{dx}{a^2+x^2}=\frac{1}{a}\operatorname{arctg}\frac{x}{a}+C$
6. $\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}=\arcsin x+C$
7. $\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{a^2-x^2}}=\arcsin\frac{x}{a}+C$
8. $\int e^x dx=e^x+C$
9. $\int a^x dx=\frac{a^x}{\ln a}+C$
10. $\int \sin xdx=-\cos x+C$
11. $\int \cos xdx=\sin x+C$
12. $\int \frac{dx}{\cos^2 x}=\operatorname{tg}x+C$
13. $\int \frac{dx}{\sin^2 x}=-\operatorname{ctg}x+C$
14. $\int \frac{dx}{\sin x}=\ln\left|\operatorname{tg}\frac{x}{2}\right|+C$
15. $\int \frac{dx}{\cos x}=\ln\left|\operatorname{tg}\left(\frac{x}{2}+\frac{\pi}{4}\right)\right|+C$
16. $\int \frac{dx}{a^2-x^2}=\frac{1}{2a}\ln\left|\frac{a+x}{a-x}\right|+C$
17. $\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{x^2\pm a^2}}=\ln\left|x+\sqrt{x^2\pm a^2}\right|+C$
